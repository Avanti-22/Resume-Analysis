What is a resume analyzer ?

The Resume Analyzer is an automated system that extracts relevant information from a job seeker's resume and provides feedback based on the job requirements.

How does the resume analyzer tool work?

It matches the resume's content and identifies the skills, education, experience, and other important information required for a job account.

How do I upload my resume to the analyzer tool?

The user will have to create an account and upload the resume for the desired jobs.The candidate resume should be in pdf, rtf or docx format.

Can the resume analyzer tool help my resume for specific job positions or industries?

Yes you can apply to various positions by adding your resumes in this tool.

Is there any data privacy or security to protect my information when using the tool?
Resumes which have been uploaded are safe and the data will be only shared with the organization not with others.

In which format should I upload my resume?

our system will be accepting the candidate resume in pdf, rtf or docx format.
